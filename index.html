About Us

Established in 2001 in the heart of the city founded by Mr B.C. Bhakuni. New Beersheba Public School is one amongst the top leading institutions of Pithoragarh managed by Aasthan Educational & Social Welfare Society. It is situated in close proximity of the hills and has a beautiful location, with neat & clean school premises. The aim of school is to provide an atmosphere to develop the individual personality of each student and to inculcate in students an awareness of their rich cultural heritage. Beersheba School is committed to provide opportunities for extracurricular activities and games for the growth of sound physical as mental health.
